FAQ's

Baggage Weight
What if I exceed my free baggage weight allowance?

Passengers carrying more than the allowed baggage limit will be charged at the baggage drop-off point.

Prohibited items
What items are not permitted on the flight?

  • Compressed gases
  • Corrosives such as acids, alkalis, mercury, batteries etc
  • Knives, scissors, Aerosols and liquids
  • Electronic devices which cannot be switched off
  • Other dangerous articles such as magnetized materials, offensive or irritating materials

Cabin Baggage
What can I carry as Cabin Baggage?

It is recommended that all medication, valuables, fragile items, perishable items and precious items (cameras, jewelry, money, electronics, etc.) should be carried in Cabin Baggage and not in Checked Baggage.

Flight Delays And Cancellations
Where do I check to find about the current status of my flight?

By clicking the 'Flight Status' option, you will be able to find any detail pretaining to the status of your flight.

Ticket Modification
How can I change/ cancel my reservation?

Visit the Edit Booking option on the homepage of our website which has a list of features that allow you to process changes/ cancellations to the itinerary and make special service request additions (meals, seat, pre-paid excess baggage etc.) in your booking.
